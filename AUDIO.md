# Audio Design Language

The audio for the WH40K Tactical Simulator must match the visual aesthetic: **grimdark, heavy, data-dense, military.** No generic sci-fi beeps or arcade pews.

## 1. UI & Tactical Sounds

UI interactions should feel like operating heavy, analog military equipment combined with high-tech auspex scanners.

| Action | Description (Sound Profile) |
|---|---|
| **Hover (Token/Pill)** | Very subtle telemetry click. Like a geiger counter registering a single tick or a camera lens focusing. |
| **Select Unit** | Solid mechanical *clack*, like flipping a heavy toggle switch. Followed by a faint, sub-audible hum while selected. |
| **Deselect** | Muted, reverse mechanical click. |
| **End Phase / Transition** | Deep, resonant metallic thud (like a blast door closing) or a very low, muffled bell toll. Needs to convey finality. |
| **Score Tick / VP** | A sharp punch card / mechanical stamp sound. |
| **Error / Illegal Move** | Dull, heavy buzzer. Not high-pitched. |

## 2. Weapon & Combat SFX

Weapon sounds in 40K are iconic. They should feel devastating and physical.

| Weapon Family | Sound Profile |
|---|---|
| **Bolt Weapons** | Distinct 2-part sound: A deep physical *thump* (the gun firing), immediately followed by a sharp, slightly delayed *crack* (the mass-reactive shell exploding). Needs heavy bass. |
| **Plasma Weapons** | High-pitch magnetic whine/charge-up, a sudden resonant release (like a thunderclap), trailing off into a cooling hiss. Hazardous fails should end in an electrical shorting explosion. |
| **Melta Weapons** | Sub-bass drop combined with the sound of a rushing blowtorch or superheated steam. A heavy, localized "whoosh" that sucks the air out of the room. |
| **Las Weapons** | A sharp, deafening whip-crack. Needs to sound like tearing air, not a generic 80s "pew-pew". |
| **Flamers** | Guttural, booming roar of ignited promethium, not an aerosol spray. Heavy, liquid fire. |
| **Chain Weapons** | Revving, sputtering two-stroke engine idle. On strike: a violent, high-RPM tearing/shredding of metal and meat. |
| **Power Weapons** | A continuous electrical drone overlaid with a sharp, ozone-cracking snap on impact. |

## 3. Movement & Units

| Class | Movement Sound |
|---|---|
| **Infantry (Astartes)** | Heavy, synchronized ceramic/metal stomps. |
| **Infantry (Horde/Ork)** | Chaotic shuffling, leathery thuds, rattling scrap. |
| **Vehicles/Walkers** | Deep servo whines, hydraulic pressure release, heavy ground-shaking footfalls (for Dreadnoughts). |
| **Hover/Fly** | Deep repulsor hums, displacing air/dust. |

## 4. Dice & Game Resolution

| Action | Sound Profile |
|---|---|
| **Dice Rolling** | Heavy, physical resin dice clattering on wood/felt. Not cheap plastic. |
| **Success/Hit** | A clean, sharp confirming ring cutting through the clatter. |
| **Enemy Save Failed** | A visceral impact sound—armor shattering or flesh tearing—syncing with the red "-1W" visual. |
| **Enemy Save Passed** | A dull, heavy ricochet/deflection *ping*, indicating the round bounced off armor. |
