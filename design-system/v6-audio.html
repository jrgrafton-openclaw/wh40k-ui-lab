1 · Background Music — Suno v5 Tracks

Music Strategy — SOTA Best Practice

  • XCOM / Chaos Gate pattern: 4-8 tracks per gameplay context (tactical, overworld, combat). Random selection per phase/turn. Crossfade on transition.
  • Adaptive layers: Base ambient drone always plays. Combat intensity layer fades in during Shooting/Charge/Fight. Fades out during Command/Movement.
  • Volume hierarchy: Music at 15-25% (background texture). SFX at 25-35% (foreground events). Never compete.
  • Phase-aware mixing: Tactical phases (Command, Deploy, Move) get ambient/drone tracks. Combat phases (Shoot, Charge, Fight) get percussive/tense tracks.
  • Current pool: 7 Suno tracks + 3 ElevenLabs originals. Goal: 8-10 curated tracks with phase-aware selection.
